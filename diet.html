3-Day Diet in Review

Overview:

All in all, this diet was pretty easy to follow. The hardest part was probably skipping the coffee on day two and day three. My overall weight loss was 6.4 pounds. My energy was a little bit lower than usual, considering the low carb intake.I had my first meal at 9:00am and my last meal at 6:00pm. I would recomend this diet for an upcoming event but not for long term.

Weightloss play-by-play

  • Day1: before eating or drinking- 134.2 lbs
  • Day 2: before eating or drinking- 131.6 lbs
  • Day 3: before eating or drinking- 129.9 lbs
  • Day 4: before eating or drinking- 127.8 lbs
