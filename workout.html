Stand-Up Paddle Board (SUP) Yoga

Well folks! It is definetly harder than it looks, but after this workout you will feel completely rejuvinated from the surface of your skin to the depths of your muscles.

Poses:

Hold each of these fabulously named yoga poses for 30 seconds on your paddleboard. Make sure to do each pose to the right and to the left. Give yourself a 30 second savasana (or "Murder Victim") between poses.
